## Why Sleep and Vitality Matter During Heatwaves in The Netherlands

Hot, restless nights can lead to fatigue, mood swings, and even irregular cycles. Sleep is the foundation of your body’s ability to recover and regulate hormones. As a sleepcoach and sleeptherapist working with women across The Netherlands, we see firsthand how disrupted sleep can worsen hormone problems, intensify PMS symptoms, and leave you feeling drained. On the other hand, quality sleep helps your body:

- Maintain healthy hormone balance  
- Boost resilience against stress  
- Restore energy and promote a positive mood  

When temperatures soar in The Netherlands, prioritizing sleep is more important than ever for women’s holistic health.

## Stress Is More Than a Feeling—It’s a Physical Response for Women

Stress isn’t just in your head. It’s a full-body response influenced by sleep, nutrition, and lifestyle choices. During a severe high temperature warning, you might notice your stress levels rising. Factors like poor sleep or processed foods can make your body produce more cortisol, the primary stress hormone. Over time, this can lead to burnout, worsened hormone problems, and lowered immunity.

But here’s the good news: stress can also be an opportunity for growth. By adopting a positive mindset—seeing challenges as chances to learn—you can actually boost beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone. This shift can help you feel more energised and balanced, even when life (or the weather) heats up.
