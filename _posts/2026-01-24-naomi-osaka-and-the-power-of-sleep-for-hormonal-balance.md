When sleep is disrupted, hormone production is thrown off, making symptoms worse and leaving you feeling drained.

### How Poor Sleep Impacts Hormones and Women’s Holistic Health

- **Increases stress hormones** like cortisol, heightening feelings of anxiety and tension.
- **Disrupts the balance** of female hormones such as estrogen and progesterone, triggering PMS or menopausal symptoms.
- **Impairs growth hormone** release, which is vital for cell repair, muscle health, and resilience.
- **Reduces DHEA**, a hormone that supports energy, mood, and immunity.

Quality sleep is essential for women’s holistic health. It fuels your body’s natural ability to restore, repair, and regulate hormones—helping you wake up with more energy and a brighter mood.
