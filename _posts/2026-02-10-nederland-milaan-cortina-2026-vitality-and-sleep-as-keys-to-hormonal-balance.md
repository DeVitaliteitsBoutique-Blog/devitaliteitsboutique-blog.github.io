## Why Vitality and Sleep Matter for Hormonal Balance in The Netherlands

Hormones are the body’s messengers, affecting everything from mood and energy to menstrual cycles and immune strength. When you’re low on vitality or sleep, these messages can get scrambled, leading to symptoms like:

- Persistent fatigue  
- Mood swings or irritability  
- Irregular cycles  
- Reduced motivation  
- Difficulty concentrating  

If these sound familiar, you’re not alone. Many women in the Netherlands—especially female entrepreneurs and those balancing work and family—struggle with sleep and hormone problems, particularly around menopause or PMS.

### How Sleep Impacts Hormones: Insights from a Dutch Sleepcoach and Sleeptherapist

Quality sleep isn’t just about feeling refreshed. As a sleepcoach and sleeptherapist can tell you, sleep is when your body resets hormone levels, repairs tissues, and builds resilience. Poor sleep can disrupt:

- Cortisol (the stress hormone), leading to anxiety and weight gain  
- Estrogen and progesterone, which regulate cycles and mood  
- DHEA and growth hormone, supporting energy and recovery  

Prioritizing better sleep is one of the most important steps you can take for hormone balance and overall vitality.
