Insomnia and restless nights can affect hormones such as cortisol, estrogen, and progesterone, especially during PMS or menopause. Supporting sleep means supporting your entire system.

When you prioritize restful sleep, you:

## Stress as a Physical and Mental Response for Dutch Women

Stress is not just a feeling—it’s a physical and mental response shaped by lifestyle choices, such as poor sleep, processed foods, or skipping meals. Chronic stress can exhaust your energy, disrupt hormone balance, and weaken your immune system. As a stress therapist and hormone expert in the Netherlands, I encourage women to view stress as a signal and an opportunity for positive change.

Embracing a positive stress mindset helps your body produce beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone, which support vitality, rejuvenation, and even slow down the effects of aging. By learning to recognize stress and respond with self-compassion, you can transform challenges into opportunities for growth and self-leadership.
