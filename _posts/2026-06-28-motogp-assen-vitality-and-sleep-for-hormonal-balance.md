## Stress as a Physical and Mental Response Affecting Hormone Balance

Stress isn’t just in your head—it’s a whole-body response that can be triggered by poor sleep, processed foods, or even pushing yourself too hard. When left unchecked, stress can drain your energy, disrupt your hormones, and worsen symptoms related to hormone problems such as PMS or menopause.

However, stress can also be a positive force. By shifting your perspective, you can view challenges as opportunities for growth. This “positive stress mindset” promotes the release of beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone, which support resilience, better sleep, and hormone balance.
