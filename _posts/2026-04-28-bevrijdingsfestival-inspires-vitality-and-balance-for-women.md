## Stress: More Than Just a Feeling for Dutch Women

Stress isn’t just mental—it’s a full-body response, influenced by everything from poor sleep to processed foods. When stress lingers, it disrupts hormone balance, often leading to symptoms like fatigue, trouble concentrating, and even immune issues. But here’s the good news: stress can also be a catalyst for growth.

**A positive stress mindset**—viewing challenges as opportunities—can actually boost beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone, supporting resilience and a sense of empowerment. This outlook is especially powerful for women balancing career, family, and personal wellbeing in the Netherlands.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What is the role of a sleepcoach in managing hormonal imbalances for women?

A sleepcoach helps women improve sleep quality, which is essential for hormone regulation. By addressing insomnia and sleep disruptions, a sleepcoach supports the production of hormones like melatonin, improving mood, immune function, and overall vitality.

### How can stress therapy help with menopause symptoms?

Stress therapy helps by teaching women techniques to manage stress responses that negatively impact hormone balance. This can reduce symptoms like mood swings, fatigue, and sleep problems commonly experienced during menopause.

### What breathing techniques does a breathing therapist recommend for better sleep?

A breathing therapist typically recommends calming practices such as diaphragmatic breathing and paced breathing exercises, which activate the parasympathetic nervous system to reduce anxiety and promote restful sleep.

### How does De Vitaliteitsboutique support women with PMS and hormone problems?
