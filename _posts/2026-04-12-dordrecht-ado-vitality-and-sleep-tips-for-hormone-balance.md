**Q2: What are common hormone problems that affect women’s sleep?**  
Hormone problems such as PMS, menopause, and thyroid imbalances can disrupt sleep patterns. These imbalances may cause insomnia, night sweats, or mood swings, all of which interfere with restful sleep and daily energy.

**Q3: How does stress impact hormone health and vitality?**  
Chronic stress elevates cortisol levels, which can disrupt hormone balance, reduce immune function, and lead to fatigue. Managing stress with the help of a stress therapist or coach can restore resilience hormones and improve vitality.

**Q5: Can breathing techniques really improve sleep and reduce stress?**  
Yes. Mindful breathing exercises activate the parasympathetic nervous system, helping to calm the mind and body. This promotes better sleep quality and lowers stress hormone levels, supporting hormone balance and well-being.
