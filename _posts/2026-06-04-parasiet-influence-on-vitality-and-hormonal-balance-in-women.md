## Understanding Parasiet and Its Hidden Effects in The Netherlands

A parasiet in the body can quietly undermine your vitality. Common symptoms—persistent tiredness, poor sleep, digestive discomfort—can overlap with signs of hormonal imbalance like PMS, menopause symptoms, or irregular cycles. When your body is battling a parasiet, immune system strength is compromised, and this stress can throw your hormones off balance.

As a sleepcoach, hormone therapist, and holistic hormone coach based in The Netherlands, we see how deeply sleep and hormones are linked. Poor sleep disrupts melatonin and cortisol rhythms, making it harder for your body to heal and restore itself. This cycle can worsen symptoms of hormonal imbalance and make recovery feel out of reach.

## The Vital Connection Between Sleep, Stress, and Hormone Balance for Women

Quality sleep isn’t just a luxury; it’s essential for women’s holistic health. Insomnia or frequent waking can leave you feeling drained, anxious, and less able to manage daily stress. This ongoing stress isn’t just emotional—it’s a physical reaction, raising cortisol levels and lowering beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone, which are crucial for resilience and vitality.

Many women notice that processed foods, irregular routines, or unresolved stress make their symptoms worse. When your sleep suffers, your hormone balance follows, leading to a cycle of fatigue, mood swings, and even burnout. That’s why burnout prevention for women starts with nurturing sleep and a positive mindset about stress.
