## The Connection Between Sleep, Vitality, and Hormones

Hormonal fluctuations often bring challenges like insomnia, irregular cycles, or low energy. As a sleepcoach and hormone expert, De Vitaliteitsboutique sees firsthand how sleep and vitality are tightly linked with hormone balance.

When you sleep well:
- Your body repairs itself, supporting hormone production and regulation  
- Stress hormones like cortisol decrease, while beneficial hormones such as growth hormone and DHEA increase  
- Mood swings and fatigue are less intense, helping you feel more resilient and joyful  

On the hottest days, our sleep can suffer due to discomfort and disrupted routines. But building a foundation of good sleep hygiene and daily vitality practices can help you navigate even 40 graden days with more ease.

## Stress Is More Than Just a Feeling

Stress is a whole-body experience, not just a mental state. Poor sleep, processed foods, and even dehydration (so common during 40 graden heat) can trigger stress responses. Over time, chronic stress can lead to hormone problems, irregular cycles, or even burnout.

But stress isn’t all bad. When managed well, it can actually be a catalyst for growth and resilience. Embracing a positive stress mindset—viewing challenges as opportunities—can boost your ‘good’ hormones like DHEA and growth hormone, building up your body’s natural reserves.
