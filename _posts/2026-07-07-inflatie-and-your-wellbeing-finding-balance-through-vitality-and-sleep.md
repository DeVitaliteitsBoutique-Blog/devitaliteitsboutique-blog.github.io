## How Inflatie Influences Your Hormones and Sleep in The Netherlands

When financial stress rises, your body responds both physically and mentally. Increased stress hormones, disrupted sleep, and changes in eating habits (like reaching for more processed foods) can quickly upset your hormone balance. This can lead to:

- Trouble falling or staying asleep (insomnia)
- Low energy and persistent fatigue
- Mood changes and irritability
- Irregular menstrual cycles or heightened PMS
- Night sweats and hot flashes during menopause

These symptoms are often signs that your sleep, stress, and hormone systems need extra support.

## The Connection Between Vitality, Sleep, and Hormone Balance for Dutch Women

Good sleep and sustained vitality are foundations for hormonal health. As a holistic hormone coach, we see that when women prioritize restful sleep and manage daily stress, their bodies naturally produce more beneficial hormones, such as DHEA and growth hormone. These hormones help:

- Repair and regenerate tissues overnight
- Balance reproductive hormones
- Support mental clarity and a positive mood
- Strengthen your immune system

But in times of inflatie, stress can become chronic, making it harder for your body to recover. That’s why focusing on practical, sustainable changes is essential for long-term wellbeing.
