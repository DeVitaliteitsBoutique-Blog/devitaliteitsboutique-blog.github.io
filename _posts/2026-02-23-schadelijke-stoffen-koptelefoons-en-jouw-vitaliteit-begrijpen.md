This can lead to symptoms such as:

- **Fatigue** that lingers even after a night’s rest  
- **Mood swings** and irritability  
- **Irregular cycles** or intensified PMS  
- **Difficulty concentrating** and feeling mentally foggy

## Stress Is More Than a Feeling—It’s Physical Too

We often think of stress as just feeling tense or overwhelmed, but it’s a real, physical response in your body. Factors like poor sleep, processed foods, or exposure to schadelijke stoffen koptelefoons can trigger stress responses. Over time, chronic stress taxes the adrenal glands, disrupts hormone production, and can even impact the immune system.

But here’s the good news: not all stress is bad! By cultivating a positive stress mindset—where challenges are seen as opportunities for growth—you can actually boost beneficial hormones. DHEA and growth hormone, for example, are released when you approach stress with confidence and self-leadership, supporting both hormonal balance and overall vitality.
