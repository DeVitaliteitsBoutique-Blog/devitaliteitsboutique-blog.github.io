- Lower immune system strength  
- Difficulty controlling cravings and energy dips  

By prioritizing sleep and vitality, you support your body’s natural rhythms and lay the groundwork for better hormone health.

## Understanding Stress and Hormones for Women in Terborg

Stress isn’t just a mental state—it’s a whole-body response. Women often juggle work, family, and self-care, making stress a familiar companion. But stress doesn’t have to be the enemy. In fact, with the right mindset, stress can help you grow stronger and more resilient.

### What Triggers Stress?

- Poor sleep or insomnia  
- Eating processed foods or skipping meals  
- Lack of movement  
- Emotional overload  

These triggers can throw your hormone balance off, making symptoms like fatigue or mood swings worse.

### Embracing a Positive Stress Mindset

Research shows that viewing stress as a challenge rather than a threat can actually boost beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone. These hormones support recovery, vitality, and a strong immune system. As your stress therapist and stress coach in Terborg, we help you reframe daily challenges and tap into your body’s natural resilience.
