## Stress, Sleep, and Your Hormones: A Dutch Perspective

Stress is more than just a feeling; it’s a physical and mental response. Chronic stress—often triggered by poor sleep, processed foods, or constant pressure—can tip your hormone balance, making symptoms worse. High stress can suppress helpful hormones while boosting cortisol, which disrupts sleep and weakens your immune system.

But here’s the good news: developing a positive stress mindset can actually boost beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone. Seeing challenges as opportunities for growth and self-leadership helps you bounce back stronger and supports a healthy hormonal balance.
