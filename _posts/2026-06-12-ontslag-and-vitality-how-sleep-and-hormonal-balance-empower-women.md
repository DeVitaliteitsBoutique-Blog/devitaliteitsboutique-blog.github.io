Losing a job, or *ontslag*, can feel like your world is suddenly turned upside down. For many women, this life event brings not just career uncertainty, but also real challenges to vitality, hormone balance, and emotional well-being. At De Vitaliteitsboutique, we understand that ontslag is more than just a career change—it’s a holistic experience that affects your whole self.

## The Impact of Ontslag on Hormones and Vitality

When facing ontslag, your body and mind respond to stress, which can disrupt sleep, increase fatigue, and trigger hormonal shifts. Hormonal imbalances—such as those related to menopause, PMS, or chronic stress—often lead to symptoms like:

- Mood swings  
- Irregular cycles  
- Insomnia  
- Low energy  
- Trouble concentrating  

These changes don’t just affect your day-to-day mood, but also your ability to recover, bounce back, and take charge of your next steps.

## Why Sleep and Stress Management Matter

Quality sleep is the foundation of hormone balance and resilience. As a sleepcoach and sleeptherapist in the Netherlands, we see firsthand how poor sleep can make symptoms of hormonal imbalance worse—especially after stressful events like ontslag. Insomnia or restless nights can lead to higher levels of the stress hormone cortisol, and disrupt the production of beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone.

Many women also find that stress increases cravings for processed foods, which can further disturb energy, sleep, and hormone health. This cycle makes it harder to recover—both physically and emotionally.

## Turning Stress Into Strength

At De Vitaliteitsboutique, we view stress as both a physical and mental response. While stress can be overwhelming, we encourage a positive stress mindset: seeing challenges like ontslag as opportunities for growth. This approach supports the release of beneficial hormones, helping you build resilience rather than feeling depleted.

Some practical ways to nurture this mindset include:

- **Breathing exercises** guided by a breathing therapist to calm the nervous system  
- Gentle movement to support mood and energy  
- Mindful nutrition choices that stabilize blood sugar and hormone levels  
- Prioritizing sleep routines to restore balance

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What is a sleepcoach and how can they help with hormonal imbalances?

A sleepcoach specializes in improving sleep quality through personalized strategies. By enhancing sleep, a sleepcoach helps regulate hormones involved in stress, mood, and metabolism, which is especially beneficial for women experiencing hormonal imbalances such as menopause or PMS.

### How does stress affect women's hormone balance and vitality?

Stress triggers the release of cortisol, which can disrupt the balance of other hormones like estrogen and progesterone. Chronic stress often leads to symptoms like insomnia, fatigue, and mood swings, impacting overall vitality. Managing stress with a stress coach or breathing therapist can restore hormone balance and energy.

### Can holistic hormone coaching support women going through menopause or PMS?

Yes, holistic hormone coaches provide tailored guidance on nutrition, movement, sleep, and stress management to support hormone balance during menopause and PMS. This approach helps alleviate symptoms naturally and promotes long-term vitality.

### What techniques are used by a breathing therapist to reduce stress?

Breathing therapists teach exercises that slow down and deepen breathing, activating the parasympathetic nervous system. This reduces anxiety, lowers cortisol levels, and improves sleep—key factors in managing stress and hormone health.
