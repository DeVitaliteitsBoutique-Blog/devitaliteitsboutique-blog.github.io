## Understanding Stress and Its Impact on Hormones for Women in The Netherlands

Stress isn’t just a mental challenge; it’s a physical response, deeply tied to your sleep and daily habits. Factors like processed foods, irregular sleep, or constant worries can send your stress hormones (like cortisol) into overdrive, affecting your menstrual cycle, mood, and even immune system strength.

But there’s good news: Stress can also have a positive side. When you approach challenges with a growth mindset, your body releases beneficial hormones such as DHEA and growth hormone. These support repair, resilience, and help balance your hormones over time.
