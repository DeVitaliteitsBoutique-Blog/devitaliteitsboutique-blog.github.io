## Stress: Challenge or Threat for Dutch Women?

Stress is more than just a feeling. It’s a physical and mental response that can be triggered by anything from a restless night to processed foods. Chronic stress can throw your hormone system off balance, leaving you feeling exhausted and anxious. But did you know that your mindset about stress matters just as much as the stress itself?

By viewing stress as a challenge rather than a threat, you can harness its positive effects. This positive stress mindset actually boosts the production of beneficial hormones such as DHEA and growth hormone, which support your body’s recovery and resilience.
