## The Connection Between Vitality, Sleep, and Hormonal Balance in Den Bosch

Hormones play a vital role in women’s health at every stage, from PMS to menopause. When hormones such as estrogen, progesterone, and cortisol become imbalanced, the effects can be far-reaching: fatigue, mood swings, irregular cycles, and even insomnia. But did you know that two of the most important factors for restoring hormone balance are **sleep** and daily vitality?

Quality sleep acts as a natural hormone reset. While you rest, your body regulates stress hormones and supports the production of growth hormone—all crucial for renewal and repair. Poor sleep, on the other hand, disrupts this delicate balance, leading to more severe symptoms and a reduced sense of well-being.
