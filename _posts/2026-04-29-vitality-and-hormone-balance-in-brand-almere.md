However, not all stress is negative. At De Vitaliteitsboutique, we encourage a positive stress mindset, helping you view challenges as opportunities for growth. This shift can actually boost beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone—supporting resilience, better mood, and a stronger immune system.
