## The Vital Connection Between Sleep, Stress, and Hormones

Sleep is much more than a nightly reset—it’s a foundation for hormone health and overall vitality. As a sleepcoach and sleeptherapist, De Vitaliteitsboutique sees firsthand how sleeping problems and insomnia can disrupt hormone balance. Poor-quality sleep can lead to imbalanced cortisol levels, leaving you feeling drained and increasing the risk of hormone problems like irregular cycles or intensified PMS symptoms.

Stress, too, plays a pivotal role. It isn’t only an emotional experience; stress is a physical and mental response influenced by many factors, including lack of sleep, processed foods, and even a busy lifestyle. Chronic stress can throw off your hormones, while positive stress—a mindset that embraces challenges as opportunities—can encourage the production of beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone. These hormones help build resilience, support immune system strength, and foster a sense of vitality.
