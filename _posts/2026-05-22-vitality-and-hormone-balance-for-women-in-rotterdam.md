When hormone balance is disrupted—by chronic stress, lack of sleep, or poor nutrition—symptoms like fatigue, mood swings, insomnia, PMS, and irregular cycles can appear. Addressing sleeping problems doesn’t just help you feel more rested—it’s a cornerstone for lasting hormonal health.

## How Stress Impacts Hormones and Energy in Women

Stress isn’t just a feeling; it’s a physical and mental response. When stress sticks around—especially without enough rest or with too many processed foods—it can throw hormones out of balance. Symptoms like irritability, fatigue, and trouble concentrating are signs your body needs support.

But stress isn’t always the enemy. At De Vitaliteitsboutique, we encourage a positive stress mindset. By viewing challenges as opportunities for growth, you can actually boost helpful hormones like DHEA and growth hormone, which protect your body and mind. Building resilience starts with understanding stress and supporting yourself—rather than trying to avoid challenges altogether.
