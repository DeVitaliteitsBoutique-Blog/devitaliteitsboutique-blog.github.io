## The Link Between Vitality, Sleep, and Hormone Balance in The Netherlands

Hormones like estrogen, progesterone, and cortisol have a significant impact on our mood, energy, and even our sleep quality. Many women notice that when they’re stressed or not sleeping well, symptoms like irritability, low energy, and irregular cycles become more intense. That’s because poor sleep and constant stress can disrupt your hormone balance, making it harder for your body to recover and thrive.

## Stress as a Physical and Mental Response for Women in The Netherlands

Stress is more than just a feeling. It’s a physical and mental reaction to daily demands, influenced by factors like insufficient sleep, processed foods, lack of movement, or a busy work-life balance. For women, unmanaged stress often shows up as headaches, fatigue, PMS, or even burnout. But here’s an empowering truth: your mindset towards stress can make all the difference.

Adopting a positive approach to stress—seeing challenges as opportunities for learning and growth—can help your body release beneficial hormones like DHEA and growth hormone. These hormones protect against the negative effects of chronic stress and contribute to resilience, vitality, and hormonal harmony.
